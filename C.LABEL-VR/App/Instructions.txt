1) Pointclouds:
Pointclouds are loaded from .pcd files in the "PointClouds" folder

Switch between them with the arrow keys left and right (Current cloud number is shown at the upper right).

2) Movement:

Use W,A,S,D for moving to the front,left,back,right.

Hold right mouse button to look around (camera will move towards the viewing direction).

Hold left shift key to move faster.

3)Labeling:

Select the desired classification group from the dropdown at the upper left

Click on a point to label it with the selected group.

Save/Load the session with Save/Load Button.
